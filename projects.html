SC22 Student Cluster Competition

Over the Spring Semester, I prepared for the student cluster competition which A&M was selected for and competed in November. It's a 48 hour competition that occurs annually at the SC conference which was in Dallas for 2022. It involved each of the selected teams to bring a cluster and compete in a series of benchmarks and it also tested our ability to run programs such as PHASTA (a Navier Stokes Equation Solver), LAMMPS (a molecular dynamics simulator), an earthquake simulating mystery program, a reproducability challenge involving dace with Python, and other programs. I worked with the HPCG benchmark and PHASTA. I optimized the values that produced the best results with the hardware we had. Link
